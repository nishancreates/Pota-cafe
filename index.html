Our Little Corner

A Cafe Born
from Quiet Love

Pota Cafe started as a tiny dream — owner Pota wanted a place where the chanting of Boudhanath mingled with the hiss of an espresso machine. Where strangers became regulars, and regulars became family.

With Chef Kamusari bringing magic to the kitchen and the finest Nepali highland beans in the hopper, we've created something rare: a cafe that feels like home the first time you walk in.
